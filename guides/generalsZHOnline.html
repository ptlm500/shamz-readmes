C&C:Online account setup

Configuration for Zero Hour

  • Follow the guide to setting up a C&C:Online account found here
  • Boot C&C Generals Zero Hour, you should see the mod splashscren, and game information displayed by GenTool
  • Select "Multiplayer", "Online"
  • Enter your email address used for C&C:Online, and use C&C:Online server details as the username/password
  • Enter your DOB (if you're under 13, you can't play C&C Generals online, go climb up a tree or something instead)
  • Connect!
